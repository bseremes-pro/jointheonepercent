Call for Research

Please help get this idea becoming widely implemented by submitting research on its impact.

Some of the questions that would welcome a short one-page paper are: Any other relevant research is welcome.

  • Illustrate this program over more companies than the small sample here
  • Test the impact of this program historically for a few companies
  • How could this 1% equally for all become a formal part of the requirement to obtain a B-Corp status?
  • Obtain support from large investors and publish their position
  • Input about the taxation implications (for various countries)
  • Political considerations (or how to avoid this idea becoming a political issue?)

Following a basic check for accuracy and quality we will publish selected research on this website with its title, a one-line summary and your name.
